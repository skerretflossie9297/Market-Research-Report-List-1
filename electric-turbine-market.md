<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Electric Turbine</li><li>Axial Flow Electric Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric turbine market is primarily divided into two types: centrifugal electric turbines and axial flow electric turbines. Centrifugal electric turbines utilize a radial design to convert kinetic energy into mechanical energy, making them suitable for applications requiring high pressure and flow rates. In contrast, axial flow electric turbines direct fluid parallel to the turbine axis, allowing for efficient energy conversion in applications with lower pressure drops. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, enhancing efficiency in power generation and mechanical processes.</p></p>
