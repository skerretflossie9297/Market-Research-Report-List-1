<p><p>The Excimer Laser System market encompasses three main types: Slit Scanning, Spot Scanning, and Wave-Front Scanning Laser Systems. Slit Scanning Laser Systems utilize a narrow slit of laser light to treat larger areas while minimizing thermal damage. Spot Scanning Laser Systems focus on individual spots for precise treatment, allowing for tailored procedures. Wave-Front Scanning Laser Systems analyze and adjust for individual eye aberrations, enhancing treatment accuracy and personalized outcomes. Each type caters to specific clinical needs in ophthalmology and dermatology.</p></p>
