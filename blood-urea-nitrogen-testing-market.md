<p><p>Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN) testing is a diagnostic procedure that measures the amount of nitrogen in the blood that comes from urea, a waste product formed from protein metabolism. The test is primarily used to assess kidney function, as elevated levels can indicate impaired kidney performance or other health issues. BUN testing plays a crucial role in diagnosing and managing conditions such as chronic kidney disease, heart failure, and dehydration.</p><p>The Blood Urea Nitrogen Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period.
