<p><p>The InGaAs image sensors market is categorized into two primary types: InGaAs linear image sensors and InGaAs area image sensors. InGaAs linear image sensors capture images in a line-by-line format, making them ideal for applications like spectroscopy and industrial scanning where high-speed and precision are crucial. In contrast, InGaAs area image sensors capture images in a two-dimensional array, suitable for applications such as thermal imaging and night vision, providing a comprehensive view of the scene. Both types cater to specialized needs in various industries.</p></p>
