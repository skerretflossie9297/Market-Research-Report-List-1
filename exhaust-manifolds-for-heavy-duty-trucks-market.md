<p><p>Exhaust manifolds for heavy-duty trucks are primarily categorized into two types: stainless steel and cast iron. Stainless steel manifolds are known for their corrosion resistance and lightweight, enhancing performance and longevity. They offer better thermal efficiency but can be more expensive. Conversely, cast iron manifolds are durable and provide excellent heat retention, often favored for their lower cost. Each type serves distinct needs within the heavy-duty trucking industry, balancing factors such as weight, durability, and thermal management.</p></p>
