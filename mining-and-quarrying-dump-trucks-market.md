<p><p>The mining and quarrying dump trucks market involves two primary types: articulated dump trucks and rigid dump trucks. Articulated dump trucks have a hinged construction that allows for better maneuverability on uneven terrain, making them ideal for off-road conditions. Rigid dump trucks, on the other hand, have a fixed chassis that provides greater stability and higher payload capacity, suited for transporting heavy materials over shorter distances. Both types play essential roles in efficient material handling in mining and quarrying operations.</p></p>
