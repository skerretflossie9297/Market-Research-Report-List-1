<p><p>The automotive air purifier market features several key types. HEPA filters capture small particles, ensuring clean air by trapping allergens and dust. Activated carbon filters effectively remove odors and VOCs through adsorption. Photocatalytic filters utilize light to catalyze reactions, breaking down pollutants into harmless substances. Ion filters release negatively charged ions to attract and neutralize airborne particles. Each type serves to enhance cabin air quality, providing a healthier driving environment and improving overall passenger comfort.</p></p>
