<p><ul><li>Inert Glass Powders</li><li>Reactive Glass Powders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental glass powders market comprises two main types: inert glass powders and reactive glass powders. Inert glass powders are typically used for their physical properties, providing strength and translucency in dental applications without contributing to any chemical reactions. Conversely, reactive glass powders participate in chemical reactions, often enhancing bonding properties and the overall functionality of dental materials. Both types play crucial roles in restorative dentistry, offering unique benefits for various applications, including fillings, seals, and prosthetics.</p></p>
