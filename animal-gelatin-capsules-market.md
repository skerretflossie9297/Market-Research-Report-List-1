<p><p>The animal gelatin capsules market is primarily divided into three types based on the source of gelatin: bovine, porcine, and piscine. Bovine gelatin is derived from cattle and is known for its high quality and stability, making it popular in dietary supplements and pharmaceuticals. Porcine gelatin, sourced from pigs, offers better solubility and lower production costs, frequently used in the food and cosmetics industries. Piscine gelatin comes from fish and is increasingly favored for kosher and halal applications, appealing to specific dietary requirements.</p></p>
