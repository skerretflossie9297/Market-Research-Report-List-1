<p><p>The medical tapes market features three main types of adhesives: solvent-based, water-based, and hot-melt adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding and durability but can have volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based adhesives are safer, less toxic, and easier to clean, making them ideal for sensitive skin applications. Hot-melt adhesives provide instant adhesion and flexibility, suitable for various medical applications. Each type caters to specific needs within the healthcare industry, balancing performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
