<p><p>The indoor humidity sensor market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless indoor humidity sensors. Wired indoor humidity sensors are connected through physical cabling, offering reliable data transmission and stability, making them suitable for fixed installations. In contrast, wireless indoor humidity sensors utilize radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology, providing flexibility and ease of installation by eliminating cabling. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including HVAC systems, smart homes, and industrial environments, ensuring optimal indoor air quality.</p></p>
