<p><p>The pocket measuring tape market is segmented into three main types: hook end, ring end, and zero end. Hook end tapes feature a retractable metal hook, ideal for accurate measurements from edges. Ring end tapes have a circular loop for easy attachment to fixed points, enhancing usability in various applications. Zero end tapes start measuring from the very tip, providing precise measurement from the start point. Each type caters to specific user needs, ensuring versatility in measuring tasks across different industries.</p></p>
