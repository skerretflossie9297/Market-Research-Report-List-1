<p><strong>The Directional Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deviated Well Directional Drilling</li><li>Horizontal Well Directional Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Directional drilling involves creating wellbores at various angles to enhance resource extraction. The deviated well directional drilling type refers to drilling at an angle from the vertical, allowing access to reservoirs that are not directly below the drilling site. In contrast, horizontal well directional drilling involves drilling horizontally along a reservoir, maximizing contact with the resource. Both methods improve drilling efficiency, access challenging locations, and reduce surface disruption, playing crucial roles in the oil and gas industry's development.</p></p>
