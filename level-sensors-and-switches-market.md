<p><p>Level sensors and switches are essential devices for measuring and monitoring fluid levels in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact level sensors directly interact with the liquid, using mechanisms like floats or probes to gauge levels. In contrast, non-contact sensors use technologies such as ultrasonic, radar, or laser to detect levels without physical interaction. Each type has its advantages, with contact sensors often being simpler and non-contact sensors offering greater versatility and reliability in challenging environments.</p></p>
