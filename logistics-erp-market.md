<p><p>In the Logistics ERP market, there are two main types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP is installed locally on a company's servers and requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. It offers enhanced control over data and security. In contrast, cloud-based ERP operates on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as users access the system through the internet. This makes it easier for logistics companies to adapt and respond to changing market demands.</p></p>
