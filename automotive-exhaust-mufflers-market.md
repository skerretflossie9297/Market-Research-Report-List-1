<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Mufflers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Mufflers</li><li>Reactive Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust mufflers market is segmented into two main types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers utilize sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise by dissipating sound waves as they pass through, making them effective for lower noise levels. Reactive mufflers, on the other hand, employ structural designs to manipulate sound waves through reflection and cancellation, providing a distinct tone and improved performance. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle comfort while minimizing noise pollution.</p></p>
