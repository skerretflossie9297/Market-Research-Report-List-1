<p><p>Semi-conductive cable tape is a specialized adhesive tape used primarily in the electrical and electronics industries for insulation and shielding purposes. The tape is typically made from materials like ethylene-propylene rubber (EPR) or cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE), which offer excellent electrical and thermal properties.</p><p>The Semi-Conductive Cable Tape Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period.
