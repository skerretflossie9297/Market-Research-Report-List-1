<p><p>Formaldehyde Free Board refers to engineered wood products that are manufactured without the use of formaldehyde-based adhesives, making them safer for indoor air quality and environmentally friendly. Common types include medium-density fiberboard (MDF), particleboard, and plywood made with alternative adhesives like soy-based or polyurethane resins.</p><p>The Formaldehyde Free Board Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer awareness about health hazards associated with formaldehyde exposure and stringent regulations promoting eco-friendly building materials.
