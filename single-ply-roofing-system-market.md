<p><p>The single-ply roofing system market includes three main types: PVC, EPDM, and TPO. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) offers durability and resistance to chemicals and UV rays, making it ideal for various climates. EPDM (rubber) is known for its flexibility and longevity, providing excellent weather resistance, particularly in low-slope applications. TPO (thermoplastic) combines thermoplastic polyolefin and rubber, offering energy efficiency and easy installation, making it increasingly popular for commercial rooftops. Each type caters to different needs based on performance, cost, and environmental factors.</p></p>
