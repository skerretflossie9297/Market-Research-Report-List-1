<p><strong>The Electrical Steel Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical steel sheets market is primarily divided into two categories: oriented electrical steel (OE) and non-oriented electrical steel (NOE). Oriented electrical steel is designed with specific grain orientations that enhance magnetic properties and efficiency in transformers and motors, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, non-oriented electrical steel has uniform magnetic properties in all directions, suitable for general-purpose applications like motors and generators. Both types play a crucial role in improving energy efficiency in electrical devices.</p></p>
