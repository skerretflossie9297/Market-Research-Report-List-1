<p><p>Retinal imaging devices encompass technologies like Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) and fundus cameras, essential for diagnosing and monitoring retinal diseases. OCT provides high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina, enabling clinicians to assess conditions such as glaucoma and macular degeneration. Fundus cameras capture detailed photographs of the retina, allowing for broad examination of the fundus. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing patient care by facilitating early detection and ongoing management of ocular health issues.</p></p>
