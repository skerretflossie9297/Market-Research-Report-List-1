<p><p>The PE substrate siliconized film market includes three main types based on polyethylene grades: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LDPE siliconized films are known for their flexibility and low moisture barrier properties, making them suitable for various applications. LLDPE films offer enhanced strength and stretch, suitable for packaging. HDPE films provide superior thickness and rigidity, often used in industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs in packaging, medical, and other sectors.</p></p>
