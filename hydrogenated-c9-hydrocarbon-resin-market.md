<p><p>The hydrogenated C9 hydrocarbon resin market comprises two main types: hydrogenated C9 petroleum resin and hydrogenated C5/C9 resin. Hydrogenated C9 petroleum resin is derived primarily from C9 fraction of petroleum, providing excellent thermal stability and adhesion properties, making it suitable for adhesives, coatings, and sealants. Conversely, hydrogenated C5/C9 resin combines C5 and C9 hydrocarbons, offering balanced performance characteristics, which enhance flexibility and durability. Both types are essential in various industries, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
