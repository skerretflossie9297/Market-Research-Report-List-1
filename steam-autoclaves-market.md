<p><p>The steam autoclaves market is segmented into traditional and tabletop types, each serving distinct needs. Traditional autoclaves are large, industrial-grade units used primarily in hospitals and laboratories for sterilizing instruments in bulk. They offer high capacity and robust features for extensive usage. In contrast, tabletop autoclaves are compact, designed for smaller facilities like dental or veterinary clinics, providing efficient sterilization in limited spaces.
