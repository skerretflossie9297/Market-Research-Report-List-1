<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane-based liquid-applied membrane market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component membranes are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, offering ease of application and minimal mixing requirements. In contrast, two-component membranes require mixing a polymer with a hardener before application, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in construction for waterproofing and protective coatings, catering to various project needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
