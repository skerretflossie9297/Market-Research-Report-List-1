<p><p>The AMLCD market is primarily categorized into three types: A-Si (Amorphous Silicon), P-Si (Polycrystalline Silicon), and others. A-Si displays are widely utilized in low-cost applications such as smartphones and LCD TVs due to their ease of manufacturing and low power consumption. P-Si, known for higher performance and better resolution, is used in premium devices including high-end displays and commercial applications. The "others" segment includes advanced technologies like IGZO and organic displays, catering to niche markets with specialized requirements.</p></p>
