<p><p>The Human Reproductive Technologies market comprises various assisted reproductive techniques catering to infertility. In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) involves fertilizing eggs outside the body and implanting embryos. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) directly injects sperm into an egg for fertilization. Zygote Intrafallopian Transfer (ZIFT) transfers fertilized eggs into the fallopian tubes. Gamete Intrafallopian Transfer (GIFT) involves placing sperm and eggs in the fallopian tubes for natural fertilization. Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) involves injecting sperm directly into the uterus. The contraceptive technology market provides solutions to prevent pregnancy.</p></p>
