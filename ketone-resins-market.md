<p><p>Ketone resins are categorized into two main types based on their chemical structure and properties. Type I ketone resins are primarily thermoplastic, exhibiting high molecular weight and excellent adhesion, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Type II ketone resins, on the other hand, are often thermosetting, providing superior chemical resistance and durability, which are ideal for applications in the automotive and aerospace industries. Both types serve diverse markets, driven by their unique performance attributes.</p></p>
