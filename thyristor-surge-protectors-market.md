<p><p>Thyristor Surge Protectors (TSPs) are specialized devices designed to protect electrical equipment from voltage spikes or transients, primarily caused by lightning strikes or switching operations. These devices utilize thyristor technology to quickly divert excess voltage away from sensitive components, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic systems. TSPs are increasingly essential in sectors such as telecommunications, industrial automation, and renewable energy, where equipment is vulnerable to power surges.</p><p>The Thyristor Surge Protectors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.
