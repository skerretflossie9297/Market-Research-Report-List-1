<p><ul><li>RF Filters</li><li>RF Switches</li><li>RF Power Amplifiers</li><li>RF Duplexer</li><li>RF Modulators & Demodulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID semiconductor devices market encompasses various components essential for radio frequency identification systems. RF filters enhance signal quality by eliminating unwanted frequencies, while RF switches enable the routing of signals within RFID systems. RF power amplifiers boost transmission power for longer range communication. RF duplexers allow simultaneous sending and receiving of signals, facilitating efficient data exchange. Lastly, RF modulators and demodulators convert data into radio signals and vice versa, enabling seamless communication in RFID applications.</p></p>
