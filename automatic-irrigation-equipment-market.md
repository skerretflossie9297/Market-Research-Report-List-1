<p><p>The automatic irrigation equipment market encompasses various key components that enhance irrigation efficiency. Flow meters measure water usage to optimize flow rates. Injectors mix fertilizers or chemicals into the water stream, ensuring proper nutrient delivery. Valves control the water flow, allowing for precise management of irrigation schedules. Sensors monitor soil moisture and weather conditions to adjust watering needs dynamically. Controllers execute pre-set irrigation programs based on data from sensors, facilitating automated and efficient watering systems for agricultural and landscape applications.</p></p>
