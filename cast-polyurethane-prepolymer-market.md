<p><p>The Cast Polyurethane Prepolymer market is primarily divided into two types: polyester-based and polyether-based prepolymers. Polyester-based prepolymers are known for their superior abrasion resistance and mechanical properties, making them ideal for applications requiring durability. In contrast, polyether-based prepolymers offer excellent water resistance and flexibility, which makes them suitable for outdoor applications and environments with humidity. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, construction, and footwear, serving specific performance and environmental needs.</p></p>
