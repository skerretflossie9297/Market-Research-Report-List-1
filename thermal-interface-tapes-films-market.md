<p><p>The thermal interface tapes and films market comprises two primary types: tapes and films. Thermal interface tapes are adhesive materials designed to enhance heat transfer between components by filling microscopic gaps, ensuring efficient thermal conductivity. They are often utilized in electronics, automotive, and industrial applications. Thermal interface films, on the other hand, provide a thin, flexible layer that also improves heat dissipation. These films are suited for applications requiring low-profile solutions, such as LED lighting and mobile devices, ensuring optimal thermal management.</p></p>
