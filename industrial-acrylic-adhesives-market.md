<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial acrylic adhesives market is primarily segmented into two types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize a water solvent, making them environmentally friendly with low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, ideal for applications requiring quick drying times and easy clean-up. In contrast, solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents that provide strong adhesion and durability, suitable for demanding environments. Both types have distinct advantages, catering to various industries such as automotive, construction, and packaging based on performance requirements.</p></p>
