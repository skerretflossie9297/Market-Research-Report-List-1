<p><p>The digital receptacle tester market includes various types designed for specific safety and functionality tests. GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) receptacle testers are used to assess the proper operation of GFCI outlets, ensuring protection against electrical shocks. AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter) receptacle testers, on the other hand, detect arc faults to prevent potential electrical fires. Both types aid electricians and homeowners in maintaining electrical safety, regulatory compliance, and ensuring the reliability of residential and commercial wiring systems.</p></p>
