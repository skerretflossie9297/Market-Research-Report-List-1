<p><ul><li>Front Drive</li><li>Rear Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elliptical trainer market features three primary types based on drive systems: front drive, rear drive, and center drive. Front drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned at the front, promoting a more pronounced incline and engaging different muscle groups. Rear drive models place the flywheel at the back, providing a natural stride and stability. Center drive machines, with pedals positioned parallel to the flywheel, offer a compact design and ergonomic movement, ensuring balanced weight distribution for a smoother workout experience.</p></p>
