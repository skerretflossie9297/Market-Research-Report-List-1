<p><p>The Automotive Proximity Sensor market includes Analog Sensors and Digital Sensors. Analog Sensors provide continuous output signals proportional to the detected object's distance, offering precise measurements for applications like parking assistance. Digital Sensors, on the other hand, deliver discrete on/off signals, ideal for detecting the presence or absence of objects, commonly used in collision avoidance systems. Both types enhance vehicle safety and automation, with analog sensors excelling in accuracy and digital sensors in simplicity and reliability for specific use cases.</p></p>
