<p><p>The aerospace composites market includes materials like glass, carbon/graphite, ceramic, and aramid composites. Glass composites are lightweight and cost-effective, used in non-structural parts. Carbon/graphite composites offer high strength-to-weight ratios, ideal for critical structural components. Ceramic composites provide exceptional heat resistance, suitable for high-temperature applications like engine parts. Aramid composites, known for impact resistance and durability, are used in interiors and protective structures. Each type enhances aircraft performance by reducing weight, improving fuel efficiency, and ensuring durability in demanding aerospace environments.</p></p>
