<p><strong>The Manual Wine Aerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Bottle Wine Aerator</li><li>Handheld Wine Aerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual wine aerator market is primarily divided into two types: in-bottle wine aerators and handheld wine aerators. In-bottle aerators are designed to be inserted directly into the bottle, allowing for seamless aeration as wine is poured. They often feature a filter to catch sediment. Handheld wine aerators, on the other hand, are used during the pouring process, enhancing aeration by drawing air into the wine. Both types improve flavor profiles and aromas, catering to wine enthusiasts' preferences.</p></p>
