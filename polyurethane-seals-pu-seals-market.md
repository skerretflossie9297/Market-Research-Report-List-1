<p><p>The polyurethane seals (PU seals) market is primarily divided into two types: non-reinforced seals and fabric-reinforced seals. Non-reinforced seals offer flexibility and cost-efficiency for applications with lower mechanical stress. They are commonly used in less demanding environments. In contrast, fabric-reinforced seals incorporate reinforcing materials to enhance strength, durability, and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-stress applications like automotive and industrial machinery. Both types cater to diverse sealing needs, balancing performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
