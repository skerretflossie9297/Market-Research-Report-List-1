<p><p>Ladle drainage sand market encompasses types such as magnesium sand, zirconium sand, and chromite sand, each serving specific foundry applications. Magnesium sand is prized for its thermal stability and low expansion, enhancing the quality of castings. Zirconium sand offers exceptional refractoriness and is used for high-temperature applications. Chromite sand, known for its high-density and low thermal expansion, is ideal for mold making and core production. Together, these sands improve casting performance and reduce defects in metal production processes.</p></p>
