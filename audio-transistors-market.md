<p><ul><li>PNP</li><li>NPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio transistors market is primarily divided into two types: PNP and NPN transistors. PNP transistors are characterized by their ability to conduct when a negative voltage is applied to the base, making them suitable for specific amplification tasks and high-side switching applications. Conversely, NPN transistors conduct when a positive voltage is applied, allowing for more straightforward integration in designs requiring low-side switching and higher current handling. This classification influences their use in various audio applications, from amplifiers to signal processing.</p></p>
