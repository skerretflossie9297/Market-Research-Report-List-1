<p><p>The axial thrust bearings market is categorized into two main types: single-direction and double-direction bearings. Single-direction bearings are designed to support axial loads in one direction, making them ideal for applications where forces are applied in a single line. In contrast, double-direction bearings can accommodate axial loads from both directions, offering greater versatility. These bearings are crucial in various industries, including automotive and aerospace, where they ensure smooth rotation and stability in rotating machinery and equipment.</p></p>
