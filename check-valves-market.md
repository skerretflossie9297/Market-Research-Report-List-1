<p><ul><li>Swing Check Valves</li><li>Lift Check Valve</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Check valves are essential devices in fluid systems, ensuring unidirectional flow and preventing backflow. The swing check valve features a hinged disc that swings open with flow and closes when it reverses, suitable for low-pressure drop applications. Lift check valves use a vertically sliding disc with a seat, ideal for high-pressure systems, providing tighter sealing. Other types, such as diaphragm and ball check valves, serve specialized functions within various industries, addressing specific operational needs and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
