<p><ul><li>Magnetic Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li><li>Self-generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recumbent exercise bikes feature various market types based on resistance and power generation technologies. Magnetic control bikes utilize magnets to create resistance, offering a quiet and smooth workout experience. Electromagnetic control bikes use electrical currents to adjust resistance levels, providing more precise and variable workouts. Self-generation bikes are powered by the user's pedaling motion, eliminating the need for external power sources and making them eco-friendly. Each type caters to different user preferences and fitness goals, enhancing versatility in workouts.</p></p>
