<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Clear Flat Glass market consists primarily of rolled glass and float glass types. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a textured surface. It's often used for architectural applications where aesthetics and light diffusion are important. Float glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth, clear surface with excellent optical properties. Both types cater to diverse applications in construction, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
