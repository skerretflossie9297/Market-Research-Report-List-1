<p><p>The capacitive touchscreen pen market is divided into two main types: active capacitive pens and passive capacitive pens. </p><p>Active capacitive pens utilize built-in electronics to provide enhanced precision and features such as pressure sensitivity and programmable buttons, making them ideal for detailed tasks like drawing or note-taking. In contrast, passive capacitive pens do not contain electronic components and rely on conductive material to interact with the touchscreen, offering a basic alternative for general navigation and simple tasks without advanced features.</p></p>
