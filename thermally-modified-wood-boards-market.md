<p><p>The thermally modified wood boards market comprises primarily two types: Thermo-S and Thermo-D. Thermo-S boards are subjected to high temperatures in a controlled environment, enhancing their durability and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, Thermo-D boards undergo a more intensive heat treatment, resulting in increased dimensional stability and improved performance in high-humidity areas. Both types offer distinct benefits, catering to specific needs in construction, interior design, and furniture manufacturing markets, promoting sustainable wood usage.</p></p>
