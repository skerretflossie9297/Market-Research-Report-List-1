<p><p>The Portable Moisture Analysers market encompasses various technologies, including Microwave, Infrared, Loss on Drying (LOD), and others. Microwave analysers utilize electromagnetic radiation to measure moisture content rapidly and non-destructively. Infrared analyzers employ infrared light to detect moisture levels based on absorption characteristics. Loss on Drying (LOD) methods gauge moisture by weighing a sample before and after drying it. Other technologies may include capacitance or resistance-based sensors, offering diverse solutions tailored to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
