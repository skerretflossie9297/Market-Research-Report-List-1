<p><strong>The Blood Sugar Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood sugar meter market comprises two primary types: standard blood glucose meters and continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems. Standard meters typically involve a fingertip blood sample to provide glucose readings at specific times, offering users a straightforward, cost-effective solution. In contrast, CGMs use a small sensor inserted under the skin to provide real-time glucose data continuously. This allows for improved monitoring and management of blood sugar levels, especially for individuals with diabetes, enhancing their ability to make timely decisions regarding their health.</p></p>
