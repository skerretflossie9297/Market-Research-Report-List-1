<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Non-Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The draining pumps market comprises two main types: submersible and non-submersible pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to operate while submerged in water, making them ideal for deep well applications and flood removal. They effectively push water to the surface. In contrast, non-submersible pumps are typically installed above water sources and rely on suction to draw water up. These pumps are suitable for applications like draining pools or removing water from basements. Both types cater to diverse drainage needs.</p></p>
