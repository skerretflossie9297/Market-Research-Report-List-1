<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Scroll Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hermetic Scroll Compressors</li><li>Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric scroll compressors market is primarily divided into two types: hermetic and semi-hermetic scroll compressors. Hermetic scroll compressors are fully sealed, integrating the motor and compressor to prevent refrigerant leakage, making them compact and efficient for various vehicle applications. In contrast, semi-hermetic scroll compressors have a removable cover, allowing for repairs and maintenance while still providing efficient cooling. Both types are essential in electric and hybrid vehicles, contributing to energy efficiency and performance in climate control systems.</p></p>
