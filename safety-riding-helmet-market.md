<p><ul><li>Full Face Helmet</li><li>Open Face Helmet</li><li>Half Helmet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety riding helmet market comprises three main types: full face, open face, and half helmets. Full face helmets provide maximum protection by covering the entire head and face, ideal for high-speed riding. Open face helmets offer a balance between protection and visibility, covering the back and top of the head while leaving the face exposed, suitable for urban riding. Half helmets, also known as brain buckets, offer minimal coverage, primarily protecting the top of the head, often favored for casual, low-speed uses.</p></p>
