<p><strong>The Disc Tube Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DTRO</li><li>DTNF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disc Tube Reverse Osmosis (DTRO) Membrane market is divided into two main types: DTRO and DTNF. DTRO systems utilize disc tube configurations to enhance water flow and reduce fouling, making them suitable for treating high turbidity water. In contrast, DTNF (Disc Tube Nano-Filtration) focuses on selectively removing smaller solutes, offering a balance between filtration efficiency and energy consumption. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, emphasizing improved efficiency and sustainability in water treatment processes.</p></p>
