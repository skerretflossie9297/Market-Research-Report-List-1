<p><p>The High Performance Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) market comprises primarily two types: gyroscopes and accelerometers. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity, allowing for precise orientation and balance in applications like aerospace and robotics. Accelerometers detect linear acceleration, which helps in motion tracking and navigation. Both components are crucial for enhancing stability and precision in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics, driving advancements in technologies such as autonomous vehicles and augmented reality systems.</p></p>
